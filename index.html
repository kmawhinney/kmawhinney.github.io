Closing the Loop

Vision is knowing where we need to go long-term - deciding where we should be in 5, 10, even 25 years.

Strategy is the roadmap to get there - prioritizing, making decisions, and organizing a clear path.

Execution is making it happen - actually building towards our vision, step-by-step.

These stages make up the loop of taking an idea and turning it into a success.

Every organization, whether a startup or a Fortune 500 company, moves through these stages. While there are many skilled people who excel in one stage, a major challenge that exists is bridging the gaps between them.

That's why my goal has always been to "close the loop" by being capable across this entire cycle, connecting vision, strategy, and execution to drive success.

Through my experience in entrepreneurship, product leadership, and software engineering, I've developed a skill set across all three stages, making me capable of bridging the gaps between them. I'm able to work as a product-minded engineer, a technically-proficient product manager, and anything in between.
